About

I'm a Michigan native living in the Bay Area of California. When I'm not plotting and arting, I'm out climbing or hiking with my dog, Echo- who I will never shut up about so don't get me started.

I've worn all hats, from bar-tending and managing in restaurants, to doing stop-motion production art, to managing two dermatology offices. I'm a multi-disciplinary powerhouse that strives for products that are self-sustaining.

I take a user-first approach, balancing research-based design with anticipating the needs of your business, as well as your clients. Let's build something distinct, timeless, and effortless.
